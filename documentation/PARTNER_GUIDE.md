## KYC Requirements

To comply with **anti-money laundering (AML)** regulations and ensure secure payouts, all partners must complete **Know Your Customer (KYC)** verification.

### Required Documents

**For Individual Accountants:**
1. **Government-issued ID:** Passport or national ID card
2. **Proof of Address:** Utility bill or bank statement (within 3 months)
3. **Professional License:** Accounting certification (if applicable)

**For Accounting Firms/Agencies:**
1. **Business Registration:** Certificate of incorporation or business license
2. **Tax Identification Number:** EMBG/EMDBS (Macedonia) or equivalent
3. **Director ID:** Government-issued ID of authorized signatory
4. **Proof of Address:** Company utility bill or bank statement

### How to Submit KYC

1. Log in to the **Partner Dashboard**
2. Go to **Settings â†’ KYC Verification**
3. Upload required documents (PDF, JPG, or PNG, max 5 MB each)
4. Click **Submit for Review**

**Review Time:** 1-2 business days

**Status:** You'll receive an email when approved. Commission payouts are only available after KYC approval.

## FAQs

### Q: How long does KYC approval take?
**A:** 1-2 business days after submitting complete documents.

### Q: Can I refer international clients (outside Macedonia)?
**A:** Yes! Facturino supports international payments via Paddle.

### Q: What if a client signs up without my link?
**A:** Contact partners@facturino.mk within 30 days of signup with proof (email, contract) and we'll manually attribute the referral.

### Q: Can I give clients a discount?
**A:** Yes. Partners can create custom discount codes in the Partner Dashboard (e.g., 10% off first 3 months).

### Q: What if a client disputes a charge and gets a refund?
**A:** Refunded amounts are deducted from your next commission payout.

### Q: Can I be a partner if I'm also a Facturino customer?
**A:** Yes! You can use Facturino for your own accounting practice and refer clients.

### Q: How do I track which clients signed up via my link?
**A:** Partner Dashboard â†’ Referrals shows all attributed signups and their status.

### Q: What happens if I stop being a partner?
**A:** You forfeit future commissions from existing referrals. However, if you rejoin within 6 months, referrals can be reinstated.
