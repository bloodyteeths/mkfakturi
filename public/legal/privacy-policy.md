## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

1. **Contract Performance** (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR):
   - To provide the Service you subscribed to
   - To process payments and send invoices

2. **Legal Obligation** (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR):
   - Tax and accounting compliance
   - Anti-money laundering (AML) checks for partners
   - Responding to lawful requests from authorities

3. **Legitimate Interest** (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR):
   - Fraud prevention and security
   - Service improvement and analytics
   - Marketing (with opt-out option)

4. **Consent** (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR):
   - PSD2 banking data access
   - Optional analytics cookies
   - Marketing communications (withdrawable at any time)

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the following rights:

### 8.1 Right to Access (Art. 15)
Request a copy of your personal data.

### 8.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 8.3 Right to Erasure (Art. 17)
Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements.

### 8.4 Right to Restriction (Art. 18)
Limit how we process your data.

### 8.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (CSV/JSON).

### 8.6 Right to Object (Art. 21)
Object to processing based on legitimate interest, including marketing.

### 8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3))
Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., for PSD2 banking access).

### 8.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint
File a complaint with the **Agency for Personal Data Protection of North Macedonia**:
- Website: https://www.privacy.mk/
- Email: info@privacy.mk

**To exercise your rights, email:** privacy@facturino.mk

## 9. Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

### 9.1 Technical Measures
- **Encryption:** All data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Control:** Role-based permissions, multi-factor authentication (admin users)
- **Regular Backups:** Daily automated backups with 30-day retention
- **Security Monitoring:** Real-time alerts for suspicious activity
- **Vulnerability Scanning:** Regular security audits and penetration testing

### 9.2 Organizational Measures
- Employee training on data protection
- Confidentiality agreements for staff
- Incident response plan
- Data breach notification procedures (within 72 hours per GDPR Art. 33)

### 9.3 Row-Level Security (RLS)
Multi-tenant database isolation ensures companies can only access their own data.
