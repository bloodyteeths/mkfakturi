# HubSpot Guide - Partner Leads

## Login
**https://app-eu1.hubspot.com** → Login with your email

---

## Pipeline (CRM → Deals → Board View)
```
New Lead → Email Verified → Emailed → Followup Due → Interested → Invite Sent → Partner Active
```

---

## Daily Tasks

### 1. Call New Leads
- Open card from **"New Lead"** column
- Call the phone number
- Verify email is correct
- Get contact person's name

### 2. Update Contact After Call
Click contact name, then set:
- **Email Verified** = Yes
- **Contact Person Name** = (their name)

### 3. Move Card
- **→ Email Verified** = Ready for email (Tamar sends)
- **→ Lost** = Wrong number / not interested

### 4. Check Hot Leads
Look at **"Followup Due"** column - these opened our email!
- **→ Interested** = Want to be partner (click Create Partner link)
- **→ Lost** = No response after 2 weeks

### 5. Create Partner Account
When deal is in **"Interested"**:
1. Open the deal card
2. Click the **"Create Partner"** link in notes
3. Done! Partner account created automatically

## Partner Performance (on deal cards)

Once a partner is active, you can see their performance on the deal card:

| Field | What it means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Commission Earned** | Total money they earned |
| **Commission Pending** | Money waiting to be paid |
| **Commission Paid** | Already paid to them |
| **Health Score** | 0-100 (higher = more active) |
| **Companies Managed** | How many clients they have |
| **Invoice Count** | Total invoices created |
| **Last Activity** | When they last did something |
| **View in Facturino** | Click to see full details |

Data updates automatically every 6 hours.

### Health Score Meaning
- **80-100** = Very active partner
- **50-79** = Normal activity
- **20-49** = Slowing down (maybe check in)
- **0-19** = Inactive (needs attention)
